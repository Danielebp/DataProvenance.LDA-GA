solomon islands at a depth of 10 km the tsunami was reported as several yards possibly ten feet high on the western side of the solomon islands and has destroyed at least two villages leaving at least four people missing and fifteen dead at least six of those who died were children the town of munda reports heavy damage from the tsunami  two villages were reported to have been completely inundated we have received reports of four people missing said solomon islands national disaster management official julian mcleod who also said that communication is difficult because most communication lines
